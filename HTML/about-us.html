Our Company
Hi, we are Phil. Fibertech Industries, lnc.,a wholly-owned
Filipino corporation established in the 90's

One part of our business involves making fiberglass
products for the industrial market.

Another unit focuses on specialized systems
for sport and institutions.
Our Entry to the market
You might wonder what does a fiberglass company
have to do with sports. This thing on the right has a lot
to do with it and it is fiberglass.

Contrary to popular belief, fiberglass is not a transparent
material. In the early 90’s and even to this date, people
confuse it for the material used in basketball
backboards.

This misconception has led us to discover and serve a
market that is very close to the hearts of a lot of
Filipinos: Basketball and Sports.
We started with a goal
We learned a lot in our experience in providing solutions
to challenging industrial projects.

It was in the 90’s and we understood how important
Basketball is for us Filipinos. We set out to design and
manufacture the finest locally-made basketball goals we
could make since 1994.
